Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish with rich meat sauce.

Ingredients: Ground beef, onion, garlic, carrots, celery, tomato paste, canned tomatoes, beef stock, spaghetti, olive oil, salt, pepper.
Instructions:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan and sauté chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and canned tomatoes, then add beef stock.
  4. Simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour until sauce thickens.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Drain pasta and serve with the Bolognese sauce on top.

Avocado Toast

Simple, healthy breakfast with smashed avocado and eggs.

Ingredients: Bread, ripe avocado, eggs, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, olive oil.
Instructions:
  1. Toast bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut avocado in half, remove pit, and scoop flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread mashed avocado on toast.
  5. Fry or poach eggs to your preference.
  6. Place eggs on top of avocado toast, sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and serve immediately.

Chicken Curry

Spicy and flavorful chicken curry with rice.

Ingredients: Chicken thighs, onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, coconut milk, tomatoes, vegetable oil, cilantro, rice.
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a large pot and sauté chopped onion until soft.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add curry powder and stir for 30 seconds to release aromas.
  4. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and add chopped tomatoes.
  6. Simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Meanwhile, cook rice according to package instructions.
  8. Garnish curry with chopped cilantro and serve with rice.
